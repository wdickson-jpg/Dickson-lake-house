Lake rules (MSLIA)

⚠️

No-wake hours

Wake-speed boating & waterskiing prohibited Sundays, Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day before 11 AM / after 7:30 PM. $100 fine.

🚤 No wake near shore

Slow no-wake within 100 ft of shore, dock, raft, swimmer.

🏊 Towing rules

Spotter + driver. 170° mirror. No towing after sunset.

🔄 Counter-clockwise

All vessels operate CCW when possible.

🧥 Life jackets

1 PFD per passenger. Kids <6 must wear one.

🔴 Jet skis

No PWC sunset–8 AM. 100 ft from docks. No one under 14.

🍻 No boating intoxicated

Same as driving. Implied consent. Severe fines.

😲 Wake liability

Owner liable for all wake damage.

🚶 Kids & boats

<12: supervised, max 35 HP. 12–15: adult or safety cert.
